FAQs on Booking

Can I track my booked bus/cab in real-time? ▼
Yes! NAVIGO provides live tracking for all booked rides from the My Bookings section after confirmation.
What are the advantages of booking with NAVIGO? ▼
AI-powered demand prediction, real-time seat availability, instant digital tickets, 24/7 support, free cancellation on select rides, and exclusive deals.
Do I need an account to book a ride? ▼
Yes, sign up for a free NAVIGO account. It takes less than a minute and gives access to booking history, e-tickets, and exclusive offers.
What is the "Booking for Women" feature? ▼
Filters rides that prioritise women's safety — showing buses/cabs with verified women-friendly operators, dedicated seating, and priority helpline access.
Where can I find my ticket after booking? ▼
Your e-ticket is in My Bookings immediately after payment and sent to your registered email.
Can I change my seat after booking? ▼
Seat changes are allowed up to 4 hours before departure, subject to availability via My Bookings → Manage → Change Seat.
Is a printed ticket required? ▼
No, a digital e-ticket shown on your phone is fully accepted. Simply show the QR code at boarding.
What payment methods are accepted? ▼
UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and wallets like Paytm and PhonePe. All secured with 256-bit SSL.
How do I apply a discount or coupon code? ▼
Enter your coupon code at checkout. Valid codes are automatically applied and the discount reflected immediately.
Is my payment information stored securely? ▼
NAVIGO never stores your card details. All processing is handled by PCI-DSS compliant gateways.
How do I cancel my booking? ▼
Go to My Bookings → select booking → Cancel. Cancellations 4+ hours before departure get a full or partial refund.
How long does a refund take? ▼
Refunds process within 5–7 business days. UPI/wallet refunds may arrive within 24 hours.
What if the operator cancels my ride? ▼
Full automatic refund within 24 hours plus proactive alternate ride options for the same route and date.
